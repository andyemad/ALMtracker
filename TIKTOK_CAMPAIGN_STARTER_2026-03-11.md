# ALM TikTok Campaign Starter

This is the first TikTok package to run with the current ALM inventory.

## Positioning

Use this line repeatedly:

`I can search 24 ALM stores for you, not just one lot.`

That is the differentiator. Most local salespeople cannot say it.

## Funnel

1. Post short vertical inventory videos.
2. Use a direct CTA in every video:
   - `DM ELANTRA`
   - `DM ROGUE`
   - `DM TRUCK`
   - `DM SUV`
3. Reply with the matching ALM link:
   - `http://localhost:5173/find?source=tiktok-organic&campaign=budget-elantra`
   - `http://localhost:5173/find?source=tiktok-organic&campaign=atl-rogue-retargeting`
   - `http://localhost:5173/find?source=tiktok-organic&campaign=truck-trade-push`
4. Let `/find` capture the lead, consent, and campaign.

## Creative Rule

Do not use fake AI car visuals as the main footage.

Use:

- real ALM vehicle photos
- real ALM lot walkarounds
- screen recordings from ALM
- AI voiceover
- AI captions
- AI background cleanup
- AI hooks and text overlays

## Posting Cadence

- 1 budget commuter video
- 1 SUV video
- 1 truck video
- 1 comment-reply video

Minimum: 4 posts per week.

## What To Measure

- DMs per video
- `/find` submissions by campaign
- contactable leads captured
- appointments booked
- sold deals later uploaded back into ALM

## What To Avoid

- fake inventory claims
- fake price claims
- fake urgency
- AI-generated car footage that does not match the real unit
- sending traffic to the generic ALM dashboard instead of `/find`
